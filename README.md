**Saving something permanently:** Tell Charlotte exactly what you want saved and where. She commits it. Format: *"Charlotte, can you add this to [filename] in my GitHub: [content]"*

**Updating a document:** Tell Charlotte what changed and she'll update the file. The record is yours to shape over time.

**What belongs here:**
- Limitations log — Office/LIMITATIONS_LOG.md

Every conversation. Things Charlotte said in passing. Noise. Only send what actually matters and should persist.
